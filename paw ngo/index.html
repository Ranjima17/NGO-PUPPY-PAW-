HOW YOU CAN HELP

Adopt community strays

Desi Indian cat and dog breeds may not be as alluring as pedigree breeds, but they’re more suited to handle the local climate and have higher immunity. They’re just as loyal too, and adopting them means letting in some extra sunshine and making room for another member of the family.

Give Donation

Your every small contribution would help atleast one animal survive. Every little penny would also pay way for their medication and even help rescue them at times of accident.

Become Volunteer

If you love animals and want to help and save them, join us become a part of our community. we can save many innocent animals by treating them and giving them a loving forever home.

Projects
Ambulance Service
PAWS responds to public call to help sick & small animal. The Ambulance caters both Large & Small Animals. PAWS ambulance covers 3 districts like Mumbai, Thane & Raigad. Ambulance service is available on Sundays also with a nominal cost.
Sterilizations & Vaccinations
PAWS started sterilization with the help of Bombay`s hospital since its starting. PAWS catches stray dogs & cats from different cities in Thane district and get them sterilized at Bombay`s Hospital. Then they release from where they are picked-up. PAWS also vaccinate against rabies to all the animals, which are sterilized.
Adoptions & Rehoming
Innumerable homeless and abandoned animals have been re-homed through our adoptions Scheme. Adoption is done after strict interviews, so that our cute puppies can have a happy forever home.
First Aid & Camps
PAWS volunteers & trustees personally go on road to treat all types of injured animals. They roam on the bikes to treat maggot wound animals and other sick animals. Some cases are referred back to the vets the next day. They remove maggots from wounds and treat animals. PAWS also arranges different first aid camps for all types of animals and also hold lectures to teach first aid to college & school students.
Legal Cruelty Cases
PAWS members & Animals Welfare Officers investigate cruelty cases, which includes rescue of animals. PAWS has filed many police complaints; FIR`s to save animals from cruelty.
Humane Education
With help of video screening, live talks & interactions, the society tries to create awareness about animal welfare. PAWS also distribute free software for dissection in schools & colleges. PAWS produces pamphlets on animal welfare acts, which are distributed free of cost to the interested citizens & volunteers.

About

WHO WE ARE

We are a nonprofit animal welfare organisation working to create a safe and healthy world for every animal, and for the people who love them. Puppy Paw is here for every animal in need, and has been since 2010. We believe that every animal deserves the best. That’s why we are dedicated to the rescue and rehabilitation of stray and abandoned animals — we believe we can transform their quality of life through kindness and compassion.

Donors

We are deeply grateful for the generous support from organisations, foundations, corporations, and individuals.

Our vision is a kind and loving world for every animal.

Tens of thousands of animals later, we’re still working hard to ensure every dog, cat, cow, horse, tortoise, rabbit is treated with love, care, and respect.
